Printing

For some users, printing a month, week, or day view may be the most important part of BORG. Some users will print the next few months and use the printout as a "daily planner" until they can get back to BORG to update it online. There is ample room on the printout to jot new appointments.

When you first try to print, the Java print service is queried to find a printer that can do the printing. It may take a few seconds before Java scans the printers on your system and pops up the printer selection box. This delay is normal.

 

The month view, day view, week view, to do list, and task list can be printed.
